Our Mission

Supply The Frontlines connects businesses seeking to make a difference with BC healthcare facilities in need. The COVID-19 pandemic has put immense strain on our healthcare system, creating resource shortages in everything from personal protective equipment to non-perishable food items.

We believe that individuals and businesses in Canada can step up to help our frontline workers by providing key essentials to our healthcare facilities. Currently, healthcare facilities are in need of personal protective equipment, non-perishable food items, toiletries, and catered meals. Our goal is to actively connect businesses seeking to help out through donations and/or discounts with healthcare facilities in British Columbia.

Get Involved

We are actively looking for businesses that want to make a difference in fighting the COVID-19 pandemic by contributing donations or discounts to healthcare facilities in British Columbia. Some resources in demand are:

  • Personal protective equipment, such as masks
  • Uniforms for healthcare workers, such as scrubs
  • Cleaning supplies, such as wipes and hand supplies
  • Catered meals, to help feed healthcare workers on the frontline
  • Individually packaged snacks and drinks for patients and workers
  • Toiletries such as shampoo and soap

Additionally, we are looking to connect with other healthcare facilities that we can help support. We are also seeking individuals who want to help out with our initiative. Contact us through email (supplythefrontlines@gmail.com) or the links below, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
